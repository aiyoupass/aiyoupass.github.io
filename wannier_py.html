wannier_py.berry_common.Fourier_Transform_RtoK(hrr, hri, arr, ari, tau, tau_cart, rVec, rVec_cart, rVec_WS, rVec_cart_WS, WS_NUM, config: Config)[source]

Performs real-space to k-space Fourier transform with different methods based on config.

Selects the appropriate Fourier transform method based on configuration settings for handling Wannier centers and Wigner-Seitz distances.

Parameters:

  • hrr – Real and imaginary parts of Hamiltonian in real space

  • hri – Real and imaginary parts of Hamiltonian in real space

  • arr – Real and imaginary parts of Berry connection in real space

  • ari – Real and imaginary parts of Berry connection in real space

  • tau – Wannier center positions in fractional coordinates

  • tau_cart – Wannier center positions in Cartesian coordinates

  • rVec – Real space lattice vectors in fractional coordinates

  • rVec_cart – Real space lattice vectors in Cartesian coordinates

  • rVec_WS – Wigner-Seitz cell vectors in fractional coordinates

  • rVec_cart_WS – Wigner-Seitz cell vectors in Cartesian coordinates

  • WS_NUM – Number of equivalent Wigner-Seitz points

  • config – Configuration object containing transform settings

Returns:

k-space transformed quantities based on selected method
